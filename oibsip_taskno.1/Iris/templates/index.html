Most irises bloom from late spring to early summer. Some bearded hybrids are remontant, meaning they may flower again later in the summer. Siberian irises tend to follow the blooming period of bearded types.

Classifying the Iris species
Blossom Iris

There are over 250 species in the genus Iris, each with its unique characteristics. The most familiar type is the tall bearded iris Iris germanica, which grows to 2-3 feet tall.These irises have six-petaled flowers with three outer hanging petals and three inner upright petals . Irises can be either bearded (with soft hairs along the center of the falls) or crested (with a comb-like ridge on the falls).
